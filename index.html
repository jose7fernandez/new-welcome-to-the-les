The Lower Eastside

The Lower East Side, also known as "Loisida", is one of New York's most historic districts. Spanning from the Bowery to the East River, and from Canal to Houston Streets, it is a must-see for all visitors who want to get a feel for old-time New York.

History

The Lower East Side is one of New York's most historic districts. Beginning in the 1840s, scores of Germans, Eastern European Jews, Poles, Russians, Ukrainians, and many others settled here. Spanning from the Bowery to the East River, and from Canal to Houston Streets, it is a must-see for all visitors who want to get a feel for old-time New York.
